## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **e-commerce user behavior data** to understand how users move through a marketing funnel:

```
Visitors (View) → Leads (Cart) → Customers (Purchase)
```

The goal is to identify **drop-off points**, measure **conversion rates**, and provide **actionable recommendations** to improve business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and structure funnel data
- Analyze Traffic-to-Lead and Lead-to-Customer conversion rates
- Identify drop-off points at each funnel stage
- Compare performance across months and product categories
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve conversions

## ✅ Recommendations

1. **Reduce Cart Abandonment** — Send reminder emails and push notifications to users who added to cart but didn't purchase
2. **Improve Product Pages** — Add better images, customer reviews, and clear CTAs to increase view-to-cart conversion
3. **Focus on Top Categories** — Invest more ad spend on high-converting product categories
4. **Simplify Checkout Process** — Reduce friction at the purchase stage to improve cart-to-purchase rate
5. **Run Targeted Promotions** — Launch offers during low-conversion periods to boost overall sales
